03 — Dress Code

What to wear

Beach Formal

We're celebrating by the ocean! Think elegant but comfortable.

Ladies: Flowing maxi dresses, elegant jumpsuits, or cocktail dresses. Wedges or block heels recommended.

Gentlemen: Linen suits, dress shirts with slacks. Ties optional. Light colors preferred.

Color Palette

Suggested Colors: Soft pastels, creams, light blues, and blush tones complement our beachside setting perfectly.

Please Avoid: White, ivory, and off-white to reserve for the bride.

Light, breathable fabrics work beautifully for our seaside ceremony.

06 — Questions

Frequently Asked

What should I wear?

+

Beach Formal attire is requested. Think elegant but comfortable for our seaside celebration. Light, breathable fabrics are perfect. See our Dress Code section for detailed guidance.

Will transportation be provided?

+

Yes! Complimentary shuttle service will be available from select hotels. Details will be included with your invitation. Valet parking is also available.

Can I bring a plus-one?

+

If your invitation includes "and guest," you're welcome to bring someone. Otherwise, we hope you'll enjoy celebrating with our other wonderful guests!

Are children welcome?

+

We've decided to make our wedding an adults-only celebration. We hope this gives parents a wonderful opportunity to enjoy a night out!

What time should I arrive?

+

Please arrive by 3:30 PM. The ceremony begins promptly at 4:00 PM. We recommend arriving early to find your seats and enjoy the pre-ceremony ambiance.

Will the ceremony be indoors or outdoors?

+

The ceremony will be outdoors with ocean views. We have a covered backup location for inclement weather. The reception will be in an elegant indoor-outdoor space.

Will there be food and drinks?

+

Yes! Cocktail hour with appetizers, followed by a three-course dinner. Open bar throughout the evening. Please inform us of dietary restrictions when you RSVP.
