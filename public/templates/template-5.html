09 — Questions

Frequently Asked

What time should I arrive?

We recommend arriving by 3:30 PM to allow time for parking and seating. The ceremony will begin promptly at 4:00 PM.

Is there parking available?

Yes, complimentary valet parking will be available at the venue. There is also a self-parking lot adjacent to the property.

Can I bring a plus-one?

Due to limited capacity, we can only accommodate guests formally invited on your invitation. If you received a plus-one, it will be indicated on your invite.

Will the ceremony be indoors or outdoors?

The ceremony will be held outdoors in the vineyard, weather permitting. We have a beautiful indoor backup plan in case of inclement weather.

Are children welcome?

While we love your little ones, we've decided to make this an adults-only celebration. We hope this gives you a chance to relax and enjoy the evening.

What dietary restrictions can be accommodated?

Please let us know about any dietary restrictions when you RSVP. Our caterer can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and most other dietary needs.

Will there be transportation provided?

We will provide shuttle service from select hotels in downtown Napa. Details will be sent with your invitation. Rideshare services are also readily available.

What is your wedding hashtag?

Share your photos using #EmmaAndJames2025. We can't wait to see the day through your eyes!
