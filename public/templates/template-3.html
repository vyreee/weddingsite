Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What should I wear?

+

Garden Formal attire is requested. Think romantic garden party with elegant touches. Flowing dresses in florals or soft pastels for ladies, light-colored suits for gentlemen. See our Dress Code section for details.

Will transportation be provided?

+

Yes! Complimentary shuttle service from select hotels and valet parking will be available. Details will be included with your invitation.

Can I bring a plus-one?

+

If your invitation includes "and guest," you're welcome to bring someone special. Otherwise, we hope you'll enjoy celebrating with our wonderful guests!

Are children welcome?

+

We've decided to make our wedding an adults-only celebration to allow parents a wonderful night out. We appreciate your understanding!

What time should I arrive?

+

Please arrive by 3:30 PM for welcome drinks in the rose garden. The ceremony begins promptly at 4:00 PM beneath the flowering arbor.

Will the ceremony be indoors or outdoors?

+

Our ceremony will be in the beautiful garden surrounded by blooms. We have a covered garden pavilion as backup for weather. The reception will be in our elegant garden terrace.

Will there be food and drinks?

+

Absolutely! Farm-to-table dinner featuring locally-sourced seasonal cuisine, plus cocktails and artisan hors d'oeuvres. Open bar throughout. Please inform us of dietary needs when you RSVP.
