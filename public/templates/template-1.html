Beach Formal Elegance

We're celebrating by the ocean! Think elegant but comfortable. Light, flowing fabrics work beautifully for our seaside ceremony.

Suggested Colors: Soft pastels, creams, light blues, and blush tones complement our beachside setting perfectly.

Ladies: Flowing maxi dresses, elegant jumpsuits, or cocktail dresses. Wedges or block heels recommended for the sand.

Gentlemen: Linen suits, dress shirts with slacks. Ties optional. Light colors preferred.
