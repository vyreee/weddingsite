Dress to Impress

Elegant coastal sophistication

What to Wear

Beach Formal

We're celebrating by the ocean, so think elegant yet comfortable. Light, breathable fabrics are perfect for our seaside setting, allowing you to look stunning while enjoying the coastal breeze.

Color Palette
Soft pastels, creams, sage green, dusty blue, and blush tones beautifully complement our beachside venue. Please kindly avoid white, ivory, and cream as these are reserved for the bride.

For Her
Flowing maxi dresses, elegant midi dresses, or sophisticated jumpsuits. We recommend wedges, block heels, or dressy sandals for comfort on the lawn and sand.

For Him
Light linen or cotton suits, dress shirts with tailored slacks, or blazers with chinos. Ties are optional but welcomed. Lighter colors work beautifully for our beach setting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our special day

What time should I arrive?

+

We recommend arriving by 3:30 PM to allow time for parking and seating. The ceremony will begin promptly at 4:00 PM.

Is there parking available?

+

Yes! We offer complimentary valet service for all guests. Simply pull up to the main entrance and our valet team will assist you.

Can I bring a plus one?

+

Due to venue capacity, we can only accommodate guests formally invited on your invitation. If you received a plus one, it will be indicated on your invitation.

Are children welcome?

+

We love your little ones, but we've decided to make our wedding an adults-only celebration. We hope this gives you a chance to enjoy a night off and celebrate with us!

What's the weather typically like in Malibu in June?

+

June in Malibu is beautiful with temperatures ranging from 65-75¬∞F (18-24¬∞C). Evenings can get cooler, so we recommend bringing a light jacket or wrap for after sunset.

Will the ceremony and reception be indoors or outdoors?

+

Both the ceremony and cocktail hour will be outdoors overlooking the ocean. The reception dinner and dancing will take place in our elegant covered pavilion, which offers the best of both worlds.

Is there a hotel room block?

+

Yes! We've reserved room blocks at several nearby hotels. Booking information and special rates were included with your invitation. Shuttle service will be provided from these hotels to the venue.

What food will be served?

+

We'll be serving a curated three-course dinner featuring locally-sourced ingredients. Menu selections will include options for various dietary preferences. Please let us know about any allergies or dietary restrictions when you RSVP.

Will there be an open bar?

+

Yes! We'll have an open bar featuring signature cocktails, wine, beer, and non-alcoholic beverages throughout the reception.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

+

We kindly ask for an unplugged ceremony‚Äîplease keep phones and cameras put away so everyone can be fully present. Our professional photographer will capture all the special moments. Feel free to take photos during the reception!
