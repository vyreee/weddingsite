07 ‚Äî Questions

Frequently Asked

What should I wear?

+

Beach Formal Elegance is requested. Think sophisticated yet comfortable for our seaside celebration. Light, flowing fabrics work beautifully. See our Details section for complete dress code guidance.

Will transportation be provided?

+

Yes! Complimentary shuttle service from select hotels and valet parking will be available. Details will be included with your invitation.

Can I bring a plus-one?

+

If your invitation includes "and guest," you're welcome to bring someone special. Otherwise, we hope you'll enjoy celebrating with our wonderful guests!

Are children welcome?

+

We've decided to make our wedding an adults-only celebration to allow parents a wonderful night out. We appreciate your understanding!

What time should I arrive?

+

Please arrive by 3:30 PM for welcome drinks and to find your seats. The ceremony begins promptly at 4:00 PM with beautiful oceanside views.

Will the ceremony be indoors or outdoors?

+

The ceremony will be outdoors with the Pacific Ocean as our backdrop. We have a beautiful covered location as backup for weather. The reception will be in an elegant indoor-outdoor space.

Will there be food and drinks?

+

Absolutely! Cocktail hour with gourmet appetizers, followed by a curated three-course meal with wine pairings. Open bar throughout the evening. Please inform us of dietary restrictions when you RSVP.
